I my scientific computing in R class, I created this project that allows users to compare literacy rates through the decades in India and Pakistan. I created an interactive map using numerous R libraries, including dplyr, shiny, and other tidyverse features.

For my programming and intro data structures class, I created a machine learning algroithm that predicted the tag attatched to comments. The algorithm was used a bag of words technique that summed the frequency of words in posts with certain tags, calculated the log-probability score of the word being used in posts with that tag, and then adding up all of the words in the new data to predict what tag was associated with the post.
